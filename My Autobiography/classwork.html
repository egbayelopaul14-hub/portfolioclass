My Autobiography

1. Intoduction

My name is Egbayelo Paul Ayomide. I was born on the 14th of October, 2008, in Agric, Ojo, Lagos State, Nigeria. I am 16 years old and currently a 200 level student of the Bells University of Technology. I come from the Yoruba ethnic group, and I am a Christian. I come from a family of seven- my parents, three brothers, one sister, and me. This is just a little about who I am.

2. Early Childhood

I spent most of my childhood in Ogun state. Despite being born in Lagos, My family moved to Ogun State when i was still little. My early childhood was pretty quiet. I wasn't the type of child that played outside a lot or had many friends. Most times, i was either watching TV indoors or doing random things.I started school at a private school, and never attended a public school throughout my primary and tertiary education. I didn't talk much in class, and I usually kept to myself, but i was good in school, and that was one of my key strenths. I was more focused in class and set on keeping myself out of trouble.

3. Education

I've attended a lot of schools since i started, some of which are; Yetty School, Majob School, Mindbuilders and Care International school, Padema School, Padema College, Blazing Glory Academy, Prime Touch College and I'm currently studying in bells University of Technology Ota.

Hobbies and Interests

When I'm not doing schoolwork, I play the keyboard. I'm a big fan of music, I love learning and playing musical instruments. I started my musical Journey about three and a half years ago, I took the bold step of learning the keyboard all by myself, although I had some help from my very good friend Sommy dan. He taught me the basics, then we continued learning and growing together till i left Blazing Glory Academy. Playing the keyboard is my most valuable asset as of today.

Dreams And Goals

I have always loved cars, I can literally look at cars from morning till night and not get tired. So i chose the closet i could get to the Automobile Industry by studying Mechanical Engineering, Although i wanted to study Automobile Engineering, the schools close to me don't offer it, so i had to choose the closest to it. I plan to major in Automotive engineering after I'm done with my first degree.

Conclusion

This is a summary of my life so far. Nothing too fancy, just the usual. This is where i am right now and I beleive my story has just begun.
